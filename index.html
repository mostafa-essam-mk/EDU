World's Biggest University

The world's biggest university is the Indira Gandhi National Open University (IGNOU) in India, with over 4 million students enrolled. Founded in 1985, IGNOU offers distance education programs across a wide range of subjects at the undergraduate and postgraduate level, as well as diploma and certificate courses. With its innovative approach to education, IGNOU has become a favored destination for those looking to pursue higher education while balancing work or other commitments. Its massive student population is supported by a vast network of regional centers and study centers across India, making it possible for students from all corners of the country to access quality education.
